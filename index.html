Clash INFO is a Discord bot about the game Clash of Clans (COC). There are many commands explained bellow : information about a Clash of Clans player, clans, the best buildings level for each TH and BH level, auto-roles systems...

If you have any question, you can join our support server! The bot is also available there for your tests before adding it in your server.

  • Clans related commands
    Clan information
    /clan_info
    Get information about your clan, with clan points, builder base points, league, required trophies, leader, number of members, clan wars stats, location, language, invitations type and description.
    Clan members
    /clan_members
    Get your clan members list, with the rank by trophies in your clan, the actual league, the best league, the town hall and the tag of each player.
    Search clan
    /search_clan
    You don't know your clan tag? Don't worry! You can search a clan with its name, and get its tag or click on it to get immediately the information given with the clan_info command.
    Super troops activated
    /clan_super_troops_activated
    You are looking for a super troop, but you don't know who in your clan has activated it? You can search a super troop in your clan and see who had activated it. It can also help you to spy your opponents during clan wars!
  • Player related commands
    Player information
    /player_info
    Get information about a player, with the possibility to choose main information, troops levels (with max level for town hall and absolute max level) or success.
    Link Clash of Clans account
    /link_coc_account
    You can link your Clash of Clans account with your Discord account, to show your Clash of Clans tag in your /member_info page
    Unlink Clash of Clans account
    /unlink_coc_account
    You can unlink your Clash of Clans account from your Discord account, if you don't want to show your Clash of Clans tag in your /member_info page
  • Clash of Clans general commands
    Buildings max level (Main village)
    /buildings_th
    Get the max level of each building for the given town hall level.
    Buildings max level (Builder base)
    /buildings_bh
    Get the max level of each building for the given builder hall level.
    Auto-roles systems
    /auto_roles th OR /auto_roles bh OR /auto_roles leagues
    Create an auto-roles system, with a message and a select menu bellow to choose which roles you want to have. There are 3 available auto-roles systems: town hall, builder hall or league
  • Discord general commands
    Member information
    /member_info
    Get information about a member, with date of joining your server, date of Discord account creation, accounts linked and member permissions in your server.
    Bot information
    /bot_info
    Get information about Clash INFO, with missing permissions in your server, the number of servers of the bot and the date of creation and certification by Discord.
